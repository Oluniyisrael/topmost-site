Academic Institutions available

Nursery

What are the goals of the nursery? The aim of the nursery is to provide a happy, safe and caring environment for all children to develop, through play and education Building Trust with Your New Little Students. Helping Kids Make Friends & Work Together. Preparing Children for Future Academic Success.

Primary

What are the goals of primary school? Building on kindergarten education, primary education continues to develop students' learning capabilities and interests, and also lays a solid foundation in students to deepen their knowledge, skills, values and attitudes when they proceed to secondary education.

Secondary

What are the goals of secondary school? Our secondary school education are to prepare students to make a positive contribution to the development of society, and to acquire attitudes of national patriotism, self-respect, self-reliance, cooperation, adaptability, and a sense of purpose and self-discipline.

Boarding

Our boarding school offers students an experience and immerse themselves in their education, grow within a supportive and diverse community, and position themselves for success after school.

Meet The Principal

A good leader always sets an excellent example. A principal should be upbeat, energetic, involved in the daily operations of the school, and receptive to the opinions of his students. Teachers, staff members, parents, students, and community members may all access an effective leader. He remains composed under pressure, considers his actions before doing them, and prioritizes the needs of the institution over his own. When gaps arise, an effective principal steps in to fill them, even if it isn't part of his typical duties.
